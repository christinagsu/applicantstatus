Information, Resources and Services for

Undergraduate Admissions

High School Students >>>

Students who have (or will have) graduated from high school and never enrolled in a college or university, unless during joint enrollment in high school

Transfer Students >>>

Students who graduated from high school and have attended a regionally accredited college or university

International Students >>>

Students who have been educated outside the U.S or students who are not United States citizens

Re-entry Students >>>

Students who previously attended Georgia State University but have not completed any coursework at Georgia State for at least three semesters

Non-traditional Students >>>

Students who have graduated from an accredited high school or earned their GED and have not been enrolled in college within the past five years

Transient Students >>>

Students who are enrolled at a regionally accredited institution in the United States and wish to attend Georgia State for credits toward a degree back at their home institution

Postbaccalaureate Students >>>

Students who have already completed a bachelor’s degree and wish to enroll in undergraduate courses at Georgia State in a non-degree seeking program

GSU-62 Students >>>

Students who are 62 or older and are Georgia residents who wish to enroll in various classes or earn a degree

High School Counselors >>>

Counselors who are advising high school students for traditional college or joint enrollment admission can find information here
